The Film Buffs Values

Everyone's welcomed to have a seat in our cinema hall, as long as they adhere to our values.

Friendship

We believe that the passion for cinema can bring together people and this can be a great start of new friendships.

Respect

Respect means that you accept somebody for who they are, even when they're different from you or you don't agree with them.

Accesible

We are encouranging people everywhere to join us in our adventure into classical cinema.

Dedication

Our members are volunteers with a passion for cinema and are dedicated to discovering new adventures.

Enjoyable

We hope everyone is having a great time being a part of The Film Buffs!

Integrity

We believe in the value of integrity; we seek to cultivate it and preserve it in The Film Buffs club.
